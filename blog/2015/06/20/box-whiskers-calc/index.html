Box_whiskers_calc

There is a shocking lack of box and whisker graph calculators on the Internet. Well, accurate ones anyway, so I made one. Originally I needed one for some calculations I was doing and made one in Python, but then I figured I can easily rewrite it into Javascript and make it available for others. I even added a simple HTML5 canvas graph. You can find it at Box and Whiskers Calculator
